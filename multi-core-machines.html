Choice

There are a parallel of options to choose from in recent times when setting up a massive multi-core or parallel machine.

The best options are considerably from Nvidia and come in various specs. Some of these are listed below for guidance but only serve as a guidance for helping you make a decision if you're confused about what to chose.

  • PNY NVIDIA GeForce GTX 650 2GB The GTX-650 has a compute capability of just 3.0 and thus does not support dynamic parallelism which is only available from GPUs with compute capability of 4.0+
  • GeForce GTX 750 Ti The GTX-750ti has a compute capability of 5.0 and thus supports dynamic paralleism and also has more cores so it will prove suitable for the purpose of this project.
